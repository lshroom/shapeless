How It Works

Step 1: Join a Team

Get invited by an existing member or join through our platform. You'll be assigned to a small team (5-10 people) focused on creating music together.

Step 2: Create Together

Use our composite music tool and Discord channels to collaborate with your team. Experiment, jam, and create your unique contribution to the movement.

Step 3: Invite 2 People Per Week

Share the movement with friends, family, and fellow peace advocates. Our viral growth model means each person inviting just 2 people per week creates exponential growth.

Step 4: Prepare for the Wave

As teams complete their music pieces, we prepare for the coordinated release. Track progress on the wave dashboard.

Step 5: Launch the Mega Wave

On launch day, all teams release their music simultaneously across all platforms. The synchronized wave creates massive awareness and invites the world to join the movement for peace.
